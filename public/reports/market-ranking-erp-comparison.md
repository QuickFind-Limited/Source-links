## Executive Summary

ERP comparison queries represent **5-8K monthly searches** and capture buyers in the active evaluation phase — the highest-intent stage before purchase. This is the most competitive SERP territory we've analyzed: vendor pages, partner pages, review platforms, and independent research sites all compete aggressively.

**Key finding**: Despite intense competition, **no single domain owns the cross-comparison narrative**. Vendors own their own comparison pages (netsuite.com/portal/solutions/intacct.shtml, acumatica.com/erp-comparison/), partners own their preferred system's comparisons, but **nobody provides a vendor-neutral, multi-system comparison with implementation cost transparency**. This is Source's opening.

**Critical insight for Source**: The comparison landscape is dominated by **biased content** — every VAR/partner publishes comparisons that favor their system. Buyers know this. The trust gap creates massive opportunity for genuinely independent, AI-powered comparison content.
