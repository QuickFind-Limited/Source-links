## Executive Summary

The QB → Sage Intacct corridor is dominated by Sage's own partner ecosystem — unlike the QB→NetSuite corridor where independent consultancies compete aggressively, Intacct's market is controlled by a network of Sage Business Partners (VARs) who resell and implement the product. The top-ranking domains are almost exclusively authorized Sage partners or Sage itself, with very few independent advisory firms ranking.

**Key findings:**
- **Sage partner network controls 70%+ of SERP real estate** — SWK Technologies, Rand Group, BT Partners, CLA (CliftonLarsonAllen), CBIZ CompuData, Vision33 dominate
- **Pricing is opaque but discoverable** — Base $9K-$12K/yr, typical SMB $15K-$35K/yr, enterprise $75K+/yr
- **Implementation costs range $10K-$150K** with 2-4 month timelines typical
- **Reddit and community content is sparse** compared to QB→NetSuite — fewer user discussions
- **Zero Source/AI-assisted migration content exists** — massive content gap
- **CLA (CliftonLarsonAllen) is the strongest single player** — 2,000+ implementations, VAR since 2001, Sage Platinum Club
