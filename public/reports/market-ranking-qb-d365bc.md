## 1. Executive Summary

The QuickBooks to Dynamics 365 Business Central migration corridor is a **high-volume, moderate-competition** market dominated by Microsoft's own documentation, a handful of large consulting firms, and a long tail of small-to-mid partners. Key findings:

- **Microsoft Learn** dominates informational SERPs with official migration documentation
- **No single consulting firm** dominates across all intent clusters — fragmented market
- **Pricing transparency is rare** — only 5 firms publish specific dollar amounts
- **YouTube content is weak** — top QB→BC migration video has <3,200 views
- **QuickBooks Desktop EOL (May 2026)** is creating urgency, driving transactional searches
- **Fixed-price migration offerings** are emerging but still uncommon
- **AI-assisted migration** content is virtually nonexistent — massive gap

### Source AI Opportunity Score: 9.2/10

The corridor presents exceptional opportunity due to: fragmented competition, zero AI migration content, weak YouTube presence, and growing urgency from QB Desktop EOL.
