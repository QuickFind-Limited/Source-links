## Executive Summary

This horizontal research spans **all major industry verticals** where "best ERP for X" queries generate significant search volume. Unlike corridor-specific research (e.g., "QB to NetSuite"), these queries capture buyers **before they've chosen a target system** — making them the highest-value content opportunities for Source's multi-corridor positioning.

**Key finding:** No single content publisher dominates across all industries. The market is fragmented across vendor sites, review aggregators, and niche consultancies — creating a massive opportunity for Source to become the single authoritative cross-industry ERP intelligence source.
