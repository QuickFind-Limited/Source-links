## Content Gap Opportunities

### Topics NOT Well-Covered by Competitors

1. **AI-assisted migration**: No competitor discusses using AI tools for data mapping, validation, or migration automation. This is a massive 2026 gap.

2. **Industry-specific migration guides**: All articles are generic. No one has written "QB to NetSuite for [manufacturing/SaaS/services/retail]" -- despite Anchor Group having industry pages.

3. **Cost breakdown transparency**: Only Ordway provides cost ranges. A detailed cost breakdown (licensing, implementation, data migration, training, ongoing) would differentiate.

4. **Real migration timelines with milestones**: No competitor provides a Gantt chart or week-by-week breakdown. Everyone says "3-6 months" without detail.

5. **Data mapping templates/tools**: No one offers downloadable templates for chart of accounts mapping, field mapping, or migration checklists.

6. **Integration-specific guides**: How to maintain Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, Shopify integrations during migration. No competitor covers this.

7. **Video walkthroughs**: No competitor has embedded video content showing the actual migration process.

8. **Customer migration stories**: Zone & Co has case studies elsewhere but no migration-specific stories. First-person accounts would be powerful.

9. **ROI calculator**: No competitor offers an interactive tool to calculate migration ROI or break-even timeline.

10. **Comparison with other ERPs**: Articles only compare QB vs NetSuite. None compare NetSuite vs Sage Intacct vs Dynamics 365 for QB graduates.

### Two Removed Pages (Fourlane + GetGSI)
Both formerly-ranking pages now return 404. This represents link equity and ranking opportunity -- any new content targeting these exact URLs/topics could capture their historical traffic and backlinks.

### AEO (AI Engine Optimization) Observations
- Anchor Group dominates AEO citations through tutorial content and review signals
- To compete for AEO, content needs: specific named experts, E-E-A-T signals, structured data, and factual specificity
- Question-answer format content has highest AEO citation potential
- Multi-step processes with numbered lists are highly citable by AI
